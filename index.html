India had a commendable performance at the Paris 2024 Olympics, securing a total of six medals: one silver and five bronze.

India has sent 117 athletes to the Paris Olympics 2024, competing across 16 sports. This includes five former Olympic medal winners – Neeraj Chopra, PV Sindhu, Mirabai Chanu, Lovlina Borgohain, and the men’s hockey team. The contingent has 70 male and 47 female athletes. The largest group is from athletics, with 29 athletes, followed by 21 shooters. Other notable Indian athletes at the Paris Games include Rohan Bopanna, Nikhat Zareen, and Manu Bhaker.
