Top 10 legendary Indian sports figures

India has produced many legendary athletes who have left a significant impact in their respective sports. Here's a list of the top 10 legendary Indian sports figures who have made their mark at the national and international levels

Sachin Tendulkar – Cricket

Tendulkar is known as the "God of Cricket." First player to score 100 international centuries.

Milkha Singh – Athletics (Track and Field)

Known as the "Flying Sikh," India’s most celebrated athletes, particularly for his performance in the 400m.

Major Dhyan Chand – Hockey

Considered the greatest hockey player of all time, Dhyan Chand led India to three Olympic gold medals (1928, 1932, 1936).

PT Usha – Athletics (Track and Field)

Popularly known as the "Payyoli Express," PT Usha is regarded as the queen of Indian track and field.

Viswanathan Anand – Chess

India's first chess Grandmaster, Anand is a five-time world chess champion.

Kapil Dev – Cricket

Captain of the Indian cricket team that won the 1983 Cricket World Cup, Kapil Dev is regarded as one of the greatest all-rounders in cricket history.

Mary Kom – Boxing

Mary Kom: 6-time World Amateur Boxing Champion.

Saina Nehwal – Badminton

Saina Nehwal was the first Indian badminton player to win an Olympic medal.

Abhinav Bindra – Shooting

Abhinav Bindra is India’s first individual Olympic gold medalist, winning gold in the 10m air rifle event at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.
